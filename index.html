Abou Me.

Hi, I'm Joana Castro, professional Web Designer & Developer.

With a bachelor's degree in architecture from the university of Minho and a degree in graphic design from the school of arts and design in Porto i have worked for 8 years creating logos, prints, marketing and website design in a dynamic and creative way, always focusing on customer satisfaction. In 2021 I decided to focus on digital and Websites. I graduated in fullstack from Flag and worked in two companies as a Web Designer & Development for 3 years. Always aiming to be a better and more complete professional, I am currently studying web design and development at LSD- Lisbon School of Design to improve my knowledge of ux / ui, programming, digital marketing and social media.
